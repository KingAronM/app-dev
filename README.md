# 🔥 Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba  
*A dark fantasy anime with breathtaking visuals, emotional depth, and intense sword battles.*

## 📘 Overview  
**Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba** is a Japanese anime based on the manga by *Koyoharu Gotouge*. Set in Taisho-era Japan, it follows a boy who joins the Demon Slayer Corps to avenge his family and cure his demon-turned sister.

> *"The bond between Nezuko and Tanjiro defies fate itself."*

- **Manga Author:** Koyoharu Gotouge  
- **Studio:** Ufotable  
- **First Aired:** April 6, 2019  
- **Genre:** Action, Supernatural, Dark Fantasy  

---

## 🗡️ Plot Summary  
Tanjiro Kamado’s family is slaughtered by demons. His sister, Nezuko, survives but is turned into one of them. To save her and eliminate the demon threat, Tanjiro joins the **Demon Slayer Corps**.

He learns the **Breathing Techniques** and takes on powerful demons in a beautifully animated, emotional journey filled with growth, loss, and unbreakable bonds.
